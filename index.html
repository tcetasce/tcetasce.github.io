ABOUT US

ASCE-TCET student forum was formed in the academic year 2016 under the core branch of Civil Engineering. The main objective of this forum was to impart additional practical knowledge to the students in the field of Civil Engineering, provide more and enhanced technical knowledge through various activities (Paper presentation, technical quiz, project competitions, seminars, workshops, etc), develop leadership skills, teamwork, confidence and all such qualities which will play a major role in their career.
